Interval functions extension. Version 1.0 November 4, 2014

canonicInterval

canonicInterval(IntervalString)

Forms a canonic interval string, consisting of a union of connected intervals sorted by order.

  • IntervalString: union of intervals as a string.
Examples
canonicInterval("[2,2^2) U (-1,3]")  // "(-1,2^2)"

intersection

intersection(arrayOfIntervals)

Forms the intersection of unions of intervals in an array.

  • arrayOfIntervals: array of strings in interval notation, e.g. "[2,5)".
Examples
intersection(array("(-2,0) U (0,2)", "[-1,1)"))  // "[-1,0) U (0,1)"
